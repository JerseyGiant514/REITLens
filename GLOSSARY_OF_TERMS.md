# REIT Lens: Glossary of Terms (The Financial Rosetta Stone)

A reference for the quantitative metrics used throughout the terminal.

## Cash Flow Metrics
*   **FFO (Funds From Operations):** Net Income + Depreciation - Gains on Sale. The standard REIT earnings metric.
*   **AFFO (Adjusted FFO):** FFO - Maintenance Capex - TI/LCs. The "True" recurring cash flow available for dividends.
*   **PAFFO (Projected AFFO):** Forward-looking AFFO estimates used for valuation.

## Operational Metrics
*   **SS-NOI (Same-Store Net Operating Income):** Income growth from properties owned for at least 12 months. Strips out acquisition noise.
*   **Occupancy:** % of portfolio leased.
*   **Leasing Spreads:** The % change in rent when a new lease replaces an old one.

## Valuation Metrics
*   **NAV (Net Asset Value):** The private market value of the real estate minus debt.
*   **Cap Rate (Capitalization Rate):** NOI / Property Value. The "Yield" of the real estate.
*   **Implied Cap Rate:** The cap rate the stock market is currently paying for the REIT's income.
*   **WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital):** The blended cost of equity and debt.

## Risk Metrics
*   **Net Debt / EBITDA:** A measure of leverage. Institutional limit is typically 6.0x - 7.0x.
*   **Fixed Charge Coverage:** EBITDA / (Interest + Preferred Dividends). Measures the "Cushion" for debt service.
*   **Maturity Wall:** The schedule of when principal debt repayments are due.
