# REIT Lens: Sector Profiles (Operational Nuance)

## Global Institutional Nuance: The "Earnings" Fallacy
Regardless of sector, institutional REIT analysis rejects GAAP **Net Income** as a primary metric. Because real estate depreciation is a non-cash expense that does not reflect economic reality, payout ratios and cash flow yields must be calculated using **AFFO** (Adjusted Funds From Operations) or **FAD** (Funds Available for Distribution). 

*   **Key Nuance:** A REIT with a 110% payout ratio on Earnings may actually have a safe 75% payout ratio on AFFO.
*   **Implementation:** All "Dividend Safety" and "Payout" logic in this terminal is anchored to AFFO/FAD.

## 1. Industrial (Logistics)
*   **Key Metric:** Mark-to-Market (MTM) Rent Spreads.
*   **Nuance:** High "In-Place" vs. "Market" rent delta due to long-term leases signed in lower-rate environments.
*   **Valuation Focus:** Implied Cap Rate vs. Replacement Cost.

## 2. Residential (Multi-family)
*   **Key Metric:** Loss-to-Lease and Occupancy Cost.
*   **Nuance:** Short lease durations (12 months) make this sector a high-frequency inflation hedge.
*   **Valuation Focus:** Nominal NAV vs. Private Market Transaction comps.

## 3. Office (The "Structural Shift" Sector)
*   **Key Metric:** Tenant Improvements (TI) and Leasing Commissions (LC) as % of NOI.
*   **Nuance:** "Economic Occupancy" is often much lower than "Physical Occupancy" due to heavy rent abatements.
*   **Valuation Focus:** Debt-to-EBITDA and the "Maturity Wall" risk.

## 4. Retail (Shopping Centers & Malls)
*   **Key Metric:** Sales PSF and Occupancy Cost Ratio.
*   **Nuance:** Anchor stability vs. Inline tenant profitability.
*   **Valuation Focus:** Dividend Safety and FFO-to-AFFO conversion.

## 5. Data Centers
*   **Key Metric:** Power Capacity (MW) and Interconnection Revenue.
*   **Nuance:** Valuation is driven by "Power Availability" more than "Square Footage."
*   **Valuation Focus:** EV/EBITDA (Growth-adjusted).

## 6. Healthcare (Senior Housing / MOB)
*   **Key Metric:** EBITDAR Coverage (Operator Health).
*   **Nuance:** Triple-net (NNN) structures mean the REIT is a "Lender" to the operator.
*   **Valuation Focus:** Credit spread over the 10Y Treasury.

## 7. Single Family Rental (SFR)
*   **Key Metric:** Bad Debt % and Turnover Cost.
*   **Nuance:** Highly granular portfolio management; sensitive to local property tax changes and "Build-to-Rent" supply.
*   **Valuation Focus:** HPI (Home Price Index) correlation and Implied Cap Rate vs. 30Y Mortgage rates.
